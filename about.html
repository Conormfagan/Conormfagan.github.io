About Me

Conor Fagan

Student Cartographer & Designer

Conor is a professional technical writer and Master's student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. With multiple years of experience as a field archaeologist and in the world of technology, Conor has honed his cartographic and geographic skills in a variety of spaces.

Conor has worked on documentation libraries accessed by hundreds of thousands of users a year. His background working in Jordan and Turkey developed his ability to work across international and multi-lingual teams, while creating and maintaining meticulous records. Conor has experience working with field mapping techniques, remote sensing, and GIS methodology within and beyond the world of archaeology.

Conor is a graduate, with honors, from the University of Missouri - Columbia and has worked for Epic Systems Corporation, Apple, and the Archaeological Expedition of Sardis.
